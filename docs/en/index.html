The Votic people

Votes (vađđa, vods) are the oldest indigenous people of the Leningrad region, today compactly living in the Kingisepp district.

History

Votes' history

The history of the Votic people began in the I—IV centuries, when near the villages of Valgovitsy and Velikino the ancestors of modern vods left the first archaeological traces of the formation of an original culture

Read more

Geography

Vods' settlements

Today, the people once given the name to the vods Pyatina of Novgorod land live mainly in the North of the Kingisepp district of the Leningrad region, in Saint Petersburg, as well as in the North-East of Estonia
